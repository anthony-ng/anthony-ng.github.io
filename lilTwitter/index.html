Lil-Twitter

Lil-Twitter is a tiny online social networking web application that enables users to send and read short 140-character messages call "lil-tweets".

Lil-Twitter let you to connect with your friends — and other fascinating people. Get in-the-moment updates on the things that interest you. And watch events unfold, in real time, from every angle.

Tech Used

Platform

Web browser

Front End

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery

Back End

Ruby, Sinatra, PostgreSQL

ACCOMPLISHMENT

Lil-Twitter let you to connect with your friends — and other fascinating people. Get in-the-moment updates on the things that interest you. And watch events unfold, in real time, from every angle.

Users can log in, and post status updates.

Users can follow other users, and see a list of their followers.

Users have their profile page, and can see others' profile page.

Users can retweet others' status updates
