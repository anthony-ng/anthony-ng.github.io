Interview Garden

Interview Garden is a social platform for preparing IT technical interviews. I strive to provide you with the best learning experience in preparing interviews for companies in the IT industry.

To be successful in landing a software engineer job offer, I believe it is mainly repeating these three important steps:

Share. Practice. Interview.

Tech Used

Platform

Web browser

Front End

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery

Back End

Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL

ACCOMPLISHMENT

Interview Garden helps you to prepare your technical interviews by connecting you with thousands of other IT professionals in the world.

You can view all recent interview questions asked by major tech companies on the main page.

If you are interested in learning more about any of the interview questions, detailed answers provided by other IT professionals are displayed after clicking the question.

You can always upvote or downvote if you like/dislike the question or answer

And of course, if you have any interview questions, and would like to know other approaches or solutions - ask here!
